Project: Raft Consensus Animator (ChatGPT Edition)
Role: Distributed systems educator.
Objective: Visualize leader election, log replication, and safety under partitions.
Design: dark terminal aesthetic with colored nodes and timelines.
Views:
- Cluster Overview: 5-node default; adjustable latency/packet loss sliders.
- Election Simulator: animate terms, votes, timeouts.
- Log Replication: show AppendEntries flow and commit index per node.
- Partition Sandbox: drag to cut links; show how Raft recovers.
- Safety Checklist: cards explaining invariants (leader completeness, log matching).
Interactions: play/pause/step; random seed display; reset cluster.
Technical: simulate with lightweight JS (no external sim libs); cap nodes to 7.
Output: only src/App.tsx, default export; optional deps noted.
