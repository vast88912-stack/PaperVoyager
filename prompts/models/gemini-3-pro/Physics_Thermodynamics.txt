**Project:** Thermodynamics & Entropy Engine (Gold Standard)
**Role:** Senior Creative Technologist.
**Objective:** Build a high-complexity, single-file React+TS teaching dashboard that simulates the Ideal Gas Law, Kinetic Molecular Theory, and Entropy.

**Global UX Spec:**
- Layout: Landing Page (Hero) -> Dashboard (Sidebar + Main + Inspector).
- Theme: Scientific Dark Mode (Thermal Map / Heat).
- Navigation: Experiment Selector.
- Inspector: "Gas State" (PV=nRT metrics).

**Module Plan (Landing + 5 Modules):**
1. **Module 0: Hero / The Arrow of Time**
   - Visual: Two separated gases (Hot/Cold) mixing irreversibly.
   - Text: "Entropy always increases. You can't unscramble an egg."

2. **Module 1: Ideal Gas Law (PV = nRT)**
   - Concept: Pressure, Volume, Temp.
   - Visualization: A Piston chamber with bouncing particles.
   - Interactive: Squeeze the Piston (Volume down) -> Pressure up. Heat it (Temp up) -> Speed up.

3. **Module 2: Maxwell-Boltzmann Distribution**
   - Concept: Speed limits.
   - Visualization: Histogram of particle speeds.
   - Simulation: Watch collisions redistribute energy. The curve emerges naturally.

4. **Module 3: Maxwell's Demon**
   - Concept: Beating Entropy?
   - Interactive: A door between two chambers.
   - Game: Manually open/close the door to trap fast particles on the right and slow on the left.
   - Metric: Temperature difference created.

5. **Module 4: Phase Change**
   - Concept: Solid, Liquid, Gas.
   - Interactive: Inter-molecular forces slider (gravity/attraction).
   - Simulation: High attraction + Low Temp = Crystal lattice (Solid). Heat it -> Melting.

6. **Module 5: The Heat Engine Challenge**
   - Task: "Carnot Cycle."
   - Goal: Manipulate Volume/Temp to extract "Work" (Area inside the PV loop).
   - Scoring: Efficiency of your cycle.

**Scoring & Gamification:**
- **Demon Slayer**: Earned by successfully separating hot/cold particles.
- **Engineer**: High efficiency engine.
