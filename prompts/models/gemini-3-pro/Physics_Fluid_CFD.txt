**Project:** Fluid Dynamics Wind Tunnel (Gold Standard)
**Role:** Senior Creative Technologist.
**Objective:** Build a high-complexity, single-file React+TS teaching dashboard that simulates fluid flow using a Lattice Boltzmann Method (LBM) or a stable Navier-Stokes solver on a grid.

**Global UX Spec:**
- Layout: Landing Page (Hero) -> Dashboard (Sidebar + Main + Inspector).
- Theme: Scientific Dark Mode (Aerospace Blue).
- Navigation: Scenario Selector (Wind Tunnel, Pipe, Obstacles).
- Inspector: "Fluid Properties" (Viscosity, Velocity, Pressure).

**Module Plan (Landing + 5 Modules):**
1. **Module 0: Hero / The Flow**
   - Visual: Colorful smoke streams flowing over a sports car silhouette.
   - Text: "The invisible forces that shape our world."

2. **Module 1: The Particle Grid (LBM)**
   - Concept: Microscopic view.
   - Visualization: Zoom in to see the "Lattice". Particles hop from cell to cell.
   - Interactive: Click a cell to add density.

3. **Module 2: The Wind Tunnel**
   - Concept: Laminar vs Turbulent.
   - Visualization: A continuous flow from left to right.
   - Interactive: Draw "Walls" or "Obstacles" (Circles, Squares) in the flow.
   - Animation: See the streamlines bend and form vortices (Von Karman Vortex Street).

4. **Module 3: Viscosity & Reynolds Number**
   - Concept: Honey vs Water.
   - Controls: Slider for Viscosity.
   - Simulation: High viscosity = smooth flow. Low viscosity = chaotic turbulence.

5. **Module 4: Pressure Field**
   - Concept: Bernoulli's Principle.
   - Visualization: Toggle "Color Mode". Show Pressure (Red=High, Blue=Low) vs Velocity.
   - Observation: Notice low pressure behind the obstacle (Drag).

6. **Module 5: The Aerodynamics Challenge**
   - Task: "Minimize Drag."
   - Goal: Draw a shape that creates the smallest "wake" (turbulence zone) behind it.
   - Scoring: Drag Coefficient calculation.

**Scoring & Gamification:**
- **Aero Engineer**: Earned by creating a streamlined shape.
- **Turbulence Master**: Earned by generating a stable Vortex Street.

**Implementation Notes:**
- **Performance**: This is the HARDEST app. Use a WebGL shader if possible, or a highly optimized 2D Canvas array loop.
- **Algorithm**: Lattice Boltzmann (D2Q9) is easiest to implement in JS grid.
- **Resolution**: Keep grid small (e.g., 100x50) for CPU performance.
