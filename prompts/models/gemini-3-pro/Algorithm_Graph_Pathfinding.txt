**Project:** Graph Pathfinding Universe (Gold Standard)
**Role:** Senior Creative Technologist.
**Objective:** Build a high-complexity, single-file React+TS teaching dashboard that deeply explains Graph Search Algorithms (Dijkstra, A*, BFS, DFS) through interactive visualizations on a grid.

**Global UX Spec:**
- Layout: Landing Page (Hero) -> Dashboard (Sidebar + Main + Inspector).
- Theme: Scientific Dark Mode (Slate/Zinc palette).
- Navigation: Sidebar with algorithm selection and tutorial steps.
- Inspector: "Pathfinder Log" showing visited nodes, fringe size, and current cost.

**Module Plan (Landing + 5 Modules):**
1. **Module 0: Hero / The Maze Runner**
   - Visual: An auto-playing demo of a swarm of particles finding a path through a maze.
   - Text: "Navigation is the heart of modern robotics and maps. See how machines 'see' the world."
   - Action: "Start Exploration" button.

2. **Module 1: Blind Search (BFS & DFS)**
   - Concept: Exploring without heuristics.
   - Controls: Grid size, Obstacle density, Animation speed.
   - Visualization: A grid where BFS expands in a circle (wavefront) vs DFS snake-like exploration.
   - Interactive: Click to place walls.

3. **Module 2: The Cost of Travel (Dijkstra)**
   - Concept: Weighted graphs (mud vs road).
   - Controls: "Paint Terrain" tool (High cost tiles).
   - Visualization: Heatmap of "Current Cost" to reach each node.
   - Simulation: Show how Dijkstra prioritizes lower cost paths, avoiding the "mud".

4. **Module 3: Heuristic Magic (A*)**
   - Concept: Guiding the search with a "Guess" (Manhattan vs Euclidean).
   - Controls: Slider for Heuristic Weight (Greedy Best-First <-> A* <-> Dijkstra).
   - Visualization: Show `f(n) = g(n) + h(n)` arrows on nodes.

5. **Module 4: Algorithm Race Arena**
   - Concept: Side-by-side comparison.
   - Layout: Split screen. Left: Dijkstra. Right: A*.
   - Simulation: Run both on the SAME random maze. Plot "Nodes Visited" vs "Path Length".
   - Goal: Show A* is faster but requires a good heuristic.

6. **Module 5: The Pathfinder Challenge**
   - Concept: Gamified test.
   - Task: "Design a maze where DFS finds the exit faster than BFS" or "Trick the Greedy algorithm".
   - Scoring: Award points for creating counter-intuitive maps.

**Scoring & Gamification:**
- **Research Points**: Earned by running simulations, reading tooltips, and winning the Challenge.
- **Progress Bar**: "Algorithm Mastery: [||||||....] 60%"
