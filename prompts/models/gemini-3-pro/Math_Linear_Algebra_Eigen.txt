**Project:** Linear Algebra Eigen-Space (Gold Standard)
**Role:** Senior Creative Technologist.
**Objective:** Build a high-complexity, single-file React+TS teaching dashboard that visualizes Linear Transformations, Eigenvalues, and Eigenvectors.

**Global UX Spec:**
- Layout: Landing Page (Hero) -> Dashboard (Sidebar + Main + Inspector).
- Theme: Scientific Dark Mode (Grid/Blueprint aesthetic).
- Navigation: Transformation Selector (Rotate, Scale, Shear, Custom).
- Inspector: "Matrix Values" and "Vector Properties".

**Module Plan (Landing + 5 Modules):**
1. **Module 0: Hero / The Matrix**
   - Visual: A 3D grid twisting and warping.
   - Text: "Matrices aren't just numbers. They are machines that stretch space."

2. **Module 1: The Transformation (2D Grid)**
   - Concept: Basis Vectors (i-hat, j-hat).
   - Interactive: Drag the basis vectors `i` and `j`.
   - Visualization: See the entire grid warp. A "F" shape follows the grid.
   - Matrix: Show the [ a b ; c d ] matrix updating in real-time.

3. **Module 2: Eigenvectors (The Invariant Axes)**
   - Concept: Vectors that don't get knocked off their span.
   - Interactive: Apply a Shear/Scale.
   - Visualization: Highlight the vector(s) that *stay on their line*.
   - Math: `Av = λv`. Show `v` (Eigenvector) and `λ` (Eigenvalue - stretch factor).

4. **Module 3: Determinant (Area Scale)**
   - Concept: Changing the size of space.
   - Interactive: Squeeze the grid until it flips (Negative Determinant).
   - Visualization: A unit square (Area=1) transforming into a parallelogram (Area=Det).

5. **Module 4: SVD (Singular Value Decomposition)**
   - Concept: Rotate -> Stretch -> Rotate.
   - Animation: Decompose a random matrix transformation into 3 distinct steps.
   - Visual: Show the unit circle turning into an ellipse.

6. **Module 5: The "Find the Eigen" Challenge**
   - Task: "Align the vector."
   - Goal: Given a hidden transformation, rotate a test vector until it stops changing direction (finding the Eigenvector manually).
   - Scoring: Accuracy of angle.

**Scoring & Gamification:**
- **Space Cadet**: Earned by exploring Determinants.
- **Eigen Hunter**: Earned by finding hidden Eigenvectors.
