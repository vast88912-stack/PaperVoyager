**Project:** Optics Lab & Ray Tracing (Gold Standard)
**Role:** Senior Creative Technologist.
**Objective:** Build a high-complexity, single-file React+TS teaching dashboard that simulates Geometric Optics (Reflection, Refraction, Lenses).

**Global UX Spec:**
- Layout: Landing Page (Hero) -> Dashboard (Sidebar + Main + Inspector).
- Theme: Scientific Dark Mode (Laser Lab / Dark Room).
- Navigation: Component Toolbox (Mirrors, Lenses, Prisms).
- Inspector: "Ray Info" (Angle of Incidence, Refractive Index).

**Module Plan (Landing + 5 Modules):**
1. **Module 0: Hero / Light is a Wave?**
   - Visual: White light hitting a prism and splitting into a rainbow.
   - Text: "Bending light to see the unseen."

2. **Module 1: Reflection (Mirrors)**
   - Concept: Angle In = Angle Out.
   - Interactive: Place Flat, Concave, and Convex mirrors.
   - Simulation: Fire laser beams. See them bounce. Focus point of concave mirror.

3. **Module 2: Refraction (Snell's Law)**
   - Concept: Bending light.
   - Interactive: Blocks of Glass/Water.
   - Controls: Refractive Index (n) slider.
   - Visual: Laser bending at the interface. Total Internal Reflection.

4. **Module 3: Lenses (Convex & Concave)**
   - Concept: Focal Length.
   - Interactive: Place lenses. Form an image of a "Candle" object.
   - Ray Diagram: Show the 3 principal rays (Parallel, Center, Focal).
   - Real vs Virtual Image visualization.

5. **Module 4: RGB Color Mixing**
   - Concept: Additive Color.
   - Interactive: 3 Spotlights (Red, Green, Blue).
   - Simulation: Move them to overlap. See Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, White.
   - Shadows: Colored shadows.

6. **Module 5: The Telescope Challenge**
   - Task: "Build a Telescope."
   - Goal: Arrange two lenses to magnify an incoming image (parallel rays).
   - Scoring: Magnification factor.

**Scoring & Gamification:**
- **Optician**: Earned by solving the telescope puzzle.
