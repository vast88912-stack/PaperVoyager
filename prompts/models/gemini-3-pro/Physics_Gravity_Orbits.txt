**Project:** Orbital Gravity Sandbox (Gold Standard)
**Role:** Senior Creative Technologist.
**Objective:** Build a high-complexity, single-file React+TS teaching dashboard that simulates N-Body Gravity, Planetary Orbits, and Slingshot maneuvers.

**Global UX Spec:**
- Layout: Landing Page (Hero) -> Dashboard (Sidebar + Main + Inspector).
- Theme: Scientific Dark Mode (Deep Space Black).
- Navigation: System Selector (Solar System, 3-Body Chaos, Galaxy).
- Inspector: "Body Info" (Mass, Velocity, Orbit predictions).

**Module Plan (Landing + 5 Modules):**
1. **Module 0: Hero / The Dance of Spheres**
   - Visual: A solar system forming from a dust cloud (accretion).
   - Text: "Gravity: The weak force that binds the universe."

2. **Module 1: Kepler's Laws**
   - Concept: Ellipses.
   - Visualization: A single planet around a sun.
   - Interactive: Change velocity to see Circle -> Ellipse -> Hyperbola (Escape).
   - Overlay: "Equal Areas in Equal Time" sweep visualization.

3. **Module 2: The Three-Body Problem**
   - Concept: Chaos.
   - Interactive: Place 3 equal mass stars.
   - Simulation: Watch the unpredictable dance.
   - Trail: Draw long orbital trails to show the complexity.

4. **Module 3: Orbital Transfer (Hohmann)**
   - Concept: Rocket Science.
   - Scenario: Earth and Mars.
   - Interactive: "Launch Rocket" button. Time your burn to catch Mars.
   - Visual: Delta-V vectors.

5. **Module 4: Galaxy Collision**
   - Concept: N-Body (Many bodies).
   - Simulation: Two disk galaxies (hundreds of small stars) merging.
   - Physics: Barnes-Hut (or simple O(N^2) for N<500). Tidal tails forming.

6. **Module 5: The Slingshot Challenge**
   - Task: "Voyager Mission."
   - Goal: Launch a probe from Earth, swing by Jupiter, and escape the solar system with max speed.
   - Scoring: Final Velocity achieved.

**Scoring & Gamification:**
- **Rocket Scientist**: Earned by successful Mars transfer.
- **Galaxy Creator**: Earned by merging galaxies.

**Implementation Notes:**
