**Project:** Monte Carlo Simulation Lab (Gold Standard)
**Role:** Senior Creative Technologist.
**Objective:** Build a high-complexity, single-file React+TS teaching dashboard that explains Monte Carlo methods: solving deterministic problems using randomness.

**Global UX Spec:**
- Layout: Landing Page (Hero) -> Dashboard (Sidebar + Main + Inspector).
- Theme: Scientific Dark Mode (Probability Green).
- Navigation: Experiment Selector.
- Inspector: "Sample Count", "Current Estimate", "Error Margin".

**Module Plan (Landing + 5 Modules):**
1. **Module 0: Hero / Rolling the Dice**
   - Visual: Thousands of dice rolling to form a perfect Gaussian Bell Curve.
   - Text: "Order emerges from large numbers. Randomness is a tool."

2. **Module 1: Estimating Pi (The Dartboard)**
   - Concept: Area ratio.
   - Visualization: A square with an inscribed circle.
   - Animation: Throw thousands of random "darts".
   - Logic: `Pi ~ 4 * (Hits / Total)`.
   - Interactive: Speed up time. Watch the digits of Pi stabilize.

3. **Module 2: Numerical Integration**
   - Concept: Finding area under weird curves.
   - Visualization: A complex function `f(x)`.
   - Animation: Random sampling points below/above the curve.
   - Comparison: Riemann Sums (Rectangles) vs Monte Carlo.

4. **Module 3: The Monty Hall Problem**
   - Concept: Conditional Probability.
   - Interactive: Play the game manually (3 doors).
   - Simulation: Run 1000 automated games with "Switch" strategy vs "Stay".
   - Result: Prove the 66% win rate for Switching.

5. **Module 4: Random Walk (Brownian Motion)**
   - Concept: Stock markets / Diffusion.
   - Visualization: 1D and 2D Random Walks.
   - Metrics: "Distance from Origin" vs `sqrt(Time)`.

6. **Module 5: The "Casino House Edge" Challenge**
   - Task: "Beat the House."
   - Goal: Design a betting strategy (Martingale, etc.) and run a Monte Carlo sim to see if you go bankrupt.
   - Result: Show that with infinite time, the House always wins (if edge > 0).

**Scoring & Gamification:**
- **Statistician Rank**: Earned by getting Pi to 3.1415...
- **Gambler**: Earned by going bankrupt in the Casino sim.
