**Project:** Chaos Theory & Strange Attractors (Gold Standard)
**Role:** Senior Creative Technologist.
**Objective:** Build a high-complexity, single-file React+TS teaching dashboard that visualizes Chaos Theory, the Butterfly Effect, and Strange Attractors (Lorenz, Rossler).

**Global UX Spec:**
- Layout: Landing Page (Hero) -> Dashboard (Sidebar + Main + Inspector).
- Theme: Scientific Dark Mode (Void/Neon Orange).
- Navigation: System Selector (Lorenz, Rossler, Double Pendulum).
- Inspector: "Phase Space" metrics and "Lyapunov Exponent".

**Module Plan (Landing + 5 Modules):**
1. **Module 0: Hero / The Butterfly Effect**
   - Visual: Two curves starting almost identical and diverging wildly.
   - Text: "Does the flap of a butterfly's wings in Brazil set off a tornado in Texas?"

2. **Module 1: The Lorenz Attractor**
   - Concept: Convection rolls.
   - Visualization: 3D (projected to 2D) trajectory of the famous "Butterfly" shape.
   - Interactive: Rotate the view. Adjust parameters (Sigma, Rho, Beta).

3. **Module 2: Sensitivity to Initial Conditions**
   - Concept: Divergence.
   - Interactive: Spawn 100 particles with tiny random offsets.
   - Animation: Watch them stay together initially, then explode apart.
   - Graph: Plot "Distance between points" over time (Log scale).

4. **Module 3: The Double Pendulum**
   - Concept: Simple physical chaos.
   - Visualization: A pendulum attached to a pendulum.
   - Trace: Draw the path of the tip. It creates beautiful messy art.
   - Energy: Show Potential vs Kinetic Energy.

5. **Module 4: Bifurcation Diagram**
   - Concept: Order to Chaos transition.
   - Visualization: The Logistic Map `x = r * x * (1-x)`.
   - Interactive: Slider for `r`. Zoom into the fractal structure of the diagram.

6. **Module 5: The "Predict the Future" Challenge**
   - Task: "Chaos Control."
   - Goal: Tweak the parameter `r` to keep the population stable for as long as possible before it goes chaotic.
   - Scoring: Time until chaos.

**Scoring & Gamification:**
- **Chaos Master**: Earned by finding the Feigenbaum constant.
- **Attractor Artist**: Earned by creating a unique Lorenz shape.
