**Project:** K-Means Clustering Viz (Gold Standard)
**Role:** Senior Creative Technologist.
**Objective:** Build a high-complexity, single-file React+TS teaching dashboard that visualizes the K-Means Clustering algorithm, Centroids, and Voronoi tessellations.

**Global UX Spec:**
- Layout: Landing Page (Hero) -> Dashboard (Sidebar + Main + Inspector).
- Theme: Scientific Dark Mode (Pastel Clusters).
- Navigation: Dataset Selector (Blobs, Circles, Moons).
- Inspector: "Cluster Metrics" (Inertia/Variance).

**Module Plan (Landing + 5 Modules):**
1. **Module 0: Hero / Finding Patterns**
   - Visual: Unlabeled grey dots instantly snapping into colorful groups.
   - Text: "Unsupervised Learning: Making sense of chaos."

2. **Module 1: The Centroids**
   - Concept: The center of gravity.
   - Interactive: Click to place K initial centroids.
   - Visual: Voronoi regions appear (colored zones).

3. **Module 2: The Assignment Step**
   - Concept: "Who is closest?"
   - Animation: Lines fly from data points to their nearest centroid. Points change color.

4. **Module 3: The Update Step**
   - Concept: Moving the center.
   - Animation: Centroids glide to the average position of their assigned points.
   - Loop: Repeat Step 2 and 3 until convergence.

5. **Module 4: The "K" Problem (Elbow Method)**
   - Concept: How many clusters?
   - Simulation: Run for K=1 to 10.
   - Chart: Plot "Inertia" (Sum of Squared Errors).
   - Interactive: Find the "Elbow" in the curve.

6. **Module 5: The Clustering Challenge**
   - Task: "Manual K-Means."
   - Goal: Drag the centroids manually to minimize the Error. Can you beat the auto-algorithm?
   - Scoring: How close to optimal Inertia?

**Scoring & Gamification:**
- **Data Scientist**: Identified the correct K.
- **Centroid Sniper**: Optimal initial placement.
